Overview

Protecting India's natural habitats and indigenous species of flora and fauna

The Habitats Trust has a special focus on the conservation of lesser-known species and habitats that are threatened, but often neglected and are in urgent need of conservation attention. Our approach is based on addressing the root cause of conservation issues, engaging with key stakeholders, and using technology for conservation. We hope to achieve conservation outcomes while also ensuring the well-being of people.

Learn more

Our Mission

We drive a concerted effort to conserve as well as restore ecological functionality for human well-being – with a lens on lesser-known species – through actions based on science, collaboration, technology, on-ground efforts, and policy advocacy.

Our Vision

We envision our natural habitats to be secured for future generations, and for the wildlife of India.
